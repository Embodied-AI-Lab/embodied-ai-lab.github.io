# Lab 1: Agentic Driving Coach

## Goal

In this lab, you will build an **AI driving coach** that reasons about traffic scenarios
and provides **safety-focused guidance** to a human driver.

Rather than controlling the vehicle directly, the agent analyzes the situation
and offers interpretable suggestions such as:

> “Slow down to increase time headway to the leading vehicle.”  
> “Change to the right lane after the adjacent car passes to reduce risk.”

## Focus

- **Agentic reasoning and decision-making** for driving scenarios  
- **Interpretable feedback** for human drivers  
- **Safety-aware planning and constraint handling**

## Learning outcomes

By the end of this lab, students should be able to:

- Model traffic situations and driver behavior using state and context
- Design coaching strategies that prioritize safety and clarity
- Evaluate driving policies using scenario-based testing

## Example components

Depending on the offering, the lab may include:

- A library of predefined traffic scenarios (e.g., highway following, merging)
- Metrics such as time-to-collision, headway, and lane-change safety scores
- A simple UI or console interface showing the coach’s advice and rationale

Implementation details, starter code, and grading rubrics will be provided on
the course LMS and/or linked from this `mdBook`.
