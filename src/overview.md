# Overview

The **Embodied Intelligence Labs** are a core experiential component of the  
**CSE 598/494 Topic: Intelligent and Safe Cyber-Physical Systems** course at  
**Arizona State University**.

Students design, implement, and evaluate intelligent agents that interact with the
physical world, with a strong emphasis on:

- safety and robustness,
- interpretability of decisions,
- and principled reasoning in cyber-physical environments.

The lab sequence currently consists of two major projects:

- **Lab 1: Agentic Driving Coach**
- **Lab 2: Safe Maneuver of Intelligent Rover**

Each lab combines algorithmic concepts with hands-on experimentation to help
students connect theory with real-world behavior of intelligent systems.
